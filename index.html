College of Applied Science, Nattika is affiliated to The University of Calicut. It is established in 2005. IHRD (Institute of Human Resources Development) came into existence on 28th January 1987. The Institute's Memorandum of Association and its rules and regulations have been judiciously framed with built-in growth provisions. The management of the Institute is vested with a Governing Body composed of ex-officio members only with the Hon'ble Minister of Education, Kerala State, as its Chairman and the Chief Secretary to the Government of Kerala as the Vice-Chairman. The functional management of the institute is controlled by an Executive Committee, headed by the Hon'ble Minister of Education, Kerala State,with the Principal secretary to Government of Kerala, Higher Education Department, as Vice-Chairman. Starting in 1987, with the institutional centre at Thiruvananthapuram and Model polytechnics in 1988 at Vadakara, IHRD has grown within a short period into a network of more than 92 institutions. In addition to regular courses of study in accadamic establishments, IHRD also runs short and long term employment oriented and continuing education programmes. IHRD College of Applied Science, Nattika is affiliated to The University of Calicut. It is established in 2005. Regular courses of B.Sc (Computer Science), BSc (Electronics),MSc(Electronics) and M.Sc (Computer Science) are offered here.
